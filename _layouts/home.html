About Me

I am a Ph.D. graduate from KAIST Computer Science, advised by Prof. Sung-Ju Lee. I have also closely collaborated with Prof. Yunxin Liu. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from KAIST in 2018.

I research on the intersection of Privacy-Preserving ML - Federated Learning (FL) and on-device NLP, uncovering novel applied machine learning technologies on smartphones and wearables. My research also focuses on promoting efficient AI deployment in practice via system-based algorithmic approaches.
